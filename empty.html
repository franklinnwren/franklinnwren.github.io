TV Series

My favorite TV series is Black Mirror. It is an amazing TV series that has mind-blowing ideas about the complex and often dark relationship between humanity and technology. Presenting plausible yet twisted future scenarios, it helps us to reflect on the ethical and moral implications of our increasingly digital world. My favorite episode is "Nosedive" (S3E1). Highly recommended!

I am also a fan of Super Pumped. Looking forward to the next series!

I USED TO BE a fan of Game of Thrones, until I watched the ending. My favorite charactor is Tyrion Lannister.

Japanese Mangas

I watch a lot of Japanese Mangas, among them, my favorite is Shin Sekai Yori (meaning: From the New World). It is almost impossible to guess how the story unfolds. The flood of information will give you a deep thought about what the existence of an "ideal" world. Also, AMAZING music.

I have been following Shingeki no Kyojin since its initial release (More than 10 years!). My favorite charactor is Erwin Smith.
